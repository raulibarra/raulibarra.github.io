Blood Brothers 2

Tactical RPG game (Unity | C#)
Senior Game Developer | DeNa Santiago
  • Developed a Fog of War system for the game that uses a custom shader to show/hide detected areas.
  • Improved the logic of their 3v3 PVP matches by loading the next player team without having to go to a full size loading screen, reducing PVP match loading times by ~50%. This system was later used for their Raid Boss event, allowing player teams to load without leaving the combat screen.
  • Developed a raid boss battle system that allowed multiple players to engage a single enemy. This was a big feature where I worked with the server team, game designers and artists to create a fun and engaging experience for players. This system featured a multi-phase boss battle where the boss would change its abilities, appearance and animations as the players dealt damage to it.
  • Programmed an horizontal re-sequencing music system that changed the music depending on the phase of the battle, where the music would change seamlessly without any audio cut.

I also composed the music and designed sound effects for the Raid Boss.
