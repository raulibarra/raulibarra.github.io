Your Love as a Gift (Unity | C#)

Concept 2D game that you can use to declare your love to your loved one.

The idea is that you can invite your partner to play this game. As soon as they reach the number of points you set, a message appears that you can customize.
The game will display a different sequence if the player chooses "Yes" or "No". Give it a try and see what happens!
The music theme also changes depending on which character you choose as your partner (try picking the zombie!).

  • Game concept, design and development.
  • Created a configurable game where you can customize the title, points to win, character sprites (you, your loved one) and a winning message.
  • Added a basic player stats system to keep track of your remaining health and score.
  • Core audio manager to play music and trigger SFXs using Unity's built-in system (it uses a pool of audio sources for better performance).
  • Created and programmed all the screens in the game.

I also composed the music and designed sound effects for the game.
