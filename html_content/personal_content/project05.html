Your Love as a Gift (Unity | C#)

Concept 2D game that you can use to declare your love to your loved one.

The idea is that you can invite your partner to play this game. As soon as they reach the set number of points, a message appears, which you can customise.
The game will display a different sequence if the player chooses "Yes" or "No". Give it a try and see what happens!
Also, the music theme will change depending on the character you select as your partner (Try picking the Zombie one!).

  • Game Concept, Design and Development.
  • Created a configurable game where you can customize the title, score to win, character sprites (you, your beloved one) and a winning message.
  • Added basic player stats system to keep track of your remaining health and score.
  • Core audio manager to play music and trigger SFXs using Unity's builtin system (It uses a pool of AudioSources for better performance).
  • Created and programmed all screens in the game.

Additionally, I composed the music and designed sound effects for the game.
