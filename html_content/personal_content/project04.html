Slippery Logs (Unity | C# | PlayFab | NodeJS)

GaaS Infinite Jumper with remote LeaderBoards and cross-play (WebGL/Android)
  • Core gameplay logic (jumping over logs, character energy, collectables and enemy)
  • Added basic player stats system to keep track of your remaining energy and score.
  • Integrated PlayFab to store player's data and retrieve LeaderBoards
    • Used custom functions to increase player score based on remote parameters.
    • While scores are simulated locally when jumping, they are calculated and retrieved by backend after X jumps to avoid cheating.
    • All parameters are stored and retrieved from PlayFab
    • Localization data is also retrieved from PlayFab.
  • Created a simple but effective pooling system for logs and collectables.
  • Created system that spawns logs sections infinitely while advancing.
  • Core audio manager to play music and trigger SFXs using Unity's builtin system (It uses a pool of AudioSources for better performance).
  • Created and programmed all screens in the game.

I also composed the music and designed the sound effects for the game.
