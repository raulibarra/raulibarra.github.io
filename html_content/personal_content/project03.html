2D Funky Platform Demo

2D Platform game (Unity | C#)
  • Core gameplay mechanics (jumping, swimming, falling rocks, collecting coins, health tracking).
  • Added camera movement (2D confinement, smooth tracking and position based camera shake using Cinemachine.
  • Added in-game sequences using Unity's timeline.
  • Added simple player stats system to keep track of your remaining lives and score.
  • Added a simple system (using Unity physics) to simulate the player being pulled by the movement of the river.
  • Created a simple dialogue system to teach the player how to play and progress.
  • Core audio manager to play music and trigger SFXs using Unity's built-in system (it uses a pool of audio sources for better performance).
  • Simple intro loop music system for in-game music.

Additionally, I composed the music and designed sound effects for the game.
