Simple FPS & MetaSounds Demo

3D FPS Game (Unreal | C++ | Blueprints | MetaSounds)
  • Created mechanics for aiming, shooting, firing projectiles and spawning impact VFXs.
  • Added a basic character stats system to keep track of damage taken and enemies killed.
  • Integrated animations with event notifiers to fire FootStep SFXs.
  • Designed enemy AI using Unreal's behaviour trees to make them chase/fire at the player.
  • Created a simple HUD system to display your health and the number of enemies left.
  • Designed a simple but effective interactive music system using MetaSounds, switching to more intense music when fighting enemies.
  • Most of the code is written in C++

Additionally, I composed the music and designed sound effects for the game.
